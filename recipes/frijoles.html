Huevo con frijoles

Description

The winner's choice. This healthy, simple meal is a great way either to start or finish your day. Scrambled eggs with beans surely has a homely flavour.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 ounces of cooked beans (any type)
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the pan
  2. Add butter to taste
  3. Add the chorizo and crumble it until done
  4. Add the eggs into the same pan and scramble them with the chorizo until done
  5. Add salt to taste
