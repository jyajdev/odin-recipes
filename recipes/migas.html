Migas

Description

The best thing to do with spare tortillas. This recipe is like a blank canvas, so it is encouraged to add salt at least. It goes with pretty much anything and can be a great foundation for more sophisticated meals (or it can just be the easiest breakfast ever).

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 5 corn tortillas
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the pan
  2. Add butter to taste
  3. Chop the tortillas into square-shaped pieces
  4. Add the pieces of tortilla and crumble it until done
  5. Add the eggs into the same pan and scramble them with the tortillas until done
  6. Add salt to taste
